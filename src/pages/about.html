About KoreaSathi

Built by students who know the struggle, for students who need a helping hand.

Our Mission

To empower international students in South Korea with accurate, up-to-date information about work permits, part-time jobs, immigration, and daily life — completely free of charge.

Our Vision

A world where no international student feels lost or exploited due to lack of information. We envision a supportive ecosystem where every student thrives in Korea.

Open Source

KoreaSathi is 100% open source. Our code is publicly available on GitHub. Anyone can contribute, audit, or fork the project. Transparency is at our core.

Who We Are

Students helping students

We are a team of international students and graduates who have experienced firsthand the challenges of living and working in Korea.

Community First

Every feature, guide, and resource is built based on real student needs. We listen to our community and prioritize what matters most.

Verified Information

We cross-check every piece of information with official Korean government sources and immigration guidelines to ensure accuracy.

Multi-Language Support

Our guides and videos are available in multiple languages to reach students from all backgrounds and nationalities.

Always Free

No hidden fees, no premium tiers. Every resource on KoreaSathi is and will always be completely free for all students.

Want to contribute?

KoreaSathi is open source. Whether you want to fix a typo, add a guide, or suggest a feature — we welcome your help.
